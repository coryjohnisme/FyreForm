FAQ

Can I just put you on staff?

We like our freedom, and we want you to be free too! No strings attached makes it so you can cancel at anytime, and not have to have consistent work requests to make it worth it.

What happens if I pause?

Once subscribed you will be billed on a monthly basis. If you need to pause for any reason, billing will be paused, and your service window will also freeze. We can stop working for up to 6 months, and then pick back up with the remaining days you have on your monthly plan.
Example: Someone is billed on the 1st. By the 10th all projects are complete, and there are no projects in the hopper. They pause their account and can resume 5 months later and get the next 20-21 days of projects covered until their next billing cycle.

What happens if I cancel?

Once subscribed you will be billed on a monthly basis. If you cancel for any reason, billing will stop and you will have the remainder of the current billing cycle to use your subscription.
Example: Someone is billed on the 1st. They get their first project returned on the 3rd. They decide to cancel. They have until the 31st (end of that billing cycle) to use their subscription as usual, but they will not be renewed for the next cycle.

Can I submit as many projects as I want?

If you’re subscribed you can submit as many projects as you want. They will be completed one-by-one, with as many revisions as needed until the subscription is paused or canceled.

Can you use my brand’s guidelines?

Of course! If you provide brand guidelines, we will stick to them. If you just want us to look at your website or past work, we can do that too. Just let us know when you submit a job.

Can you work with our current designer?

Yes and no. We work more like a lone wolf. We will follow the requests of the submission, without further direct collaboration. However, project files can be delivered for your designer to continue work on. Also, if you have a project you’ve done in the past or that needs to be changed or completed, and you can provide the project files, we can usually accommodate.

What if I’m not happy with the design?

Request a revision, and another one, and another one, until you are completely satisfied with the result... no hard feelings.

Do you offer refunds?

Refunds are not available due to the substantial work invested in each design. Plans can be paused or canceled at anytime, though.
